### **Name: Silas Vance**

**Alias:** Apex

### **Origin**

A catastrophic failure in a resonance chamber fused experimental kinetic-dampening metamaterials into his cellular structure, triggering hyper-accelerated cellular evolution.

### **Abilities**

* **Kinetic Absorption:** Absorbs kinetic energy from physical impacts, ballistics, and explosions. This stored energy is redirected as targeted concussive shockwaves.
* **Biological Adaptation:** Cells autonomously rewrite their DNA in real-time to counter environmental threats. Grants instantaneous temporary mutations, such as thermal resistance in fire, toxin immunity, and rapid tissue regeneration.

### **Weakness**

Cellular structure becomes highly unstable if completely deprived of kinetic input or isolated in sensory-deprivation environments.
